Our Services

Comprehensive cancer care designed for you, with you

Your Partner in Cancer Care

Welcome to our comprehensive cancer services. With over 15 years of specialized oncology experience, Dr. Jonathan Callister and our dedicated team provide personalized, evidence-based cancer care that addresses your unique needs. We combine cutting-edge medical treatments with compassionate support to guide you through every step of your journey.

Our approach centers on you as a whole person - not just your diagnosis. Our Comprehensive Cancer Services

Specialized care for every stage of your cancer journey

Comprehensive Cancer Diagnosis

Accurate diagnosis is the foundation of effective cancer treatment. We utilize advanced imaging technologies, precision biopsies, and expert pathology services to provide definitive diagnoses. Our team offers second opinion consultations to ensure diagnostic confidence before treatment begins.

Individualized Treatment Planning

Every cancer journey is unique. Our multidisciplinary team reviews each case to develop personalized treatment strategies. We incorporate genetic profiling, comprehensive staging, and your personal preferences to create a roadmap tailored to your specific situation and goals.

Medical Oncology

Our medical oncology services include chemotherapy, targeted therapies, and hormone treatments. We use the latest evidence-based protocols while prioritizing your quality of life. Our supportive care team manages side effects to help you maintain your daily activities during treatment.

Radiation Oncology

We offer advanced radiation options including IMRT, stereotactic radiosurgery, and brachytherapy. Our precision techniques target cancer cells while sparing healthy tissue. Treatment plans are customized to your tumor type, location, and overall health profile.

Surgical Oncology

Our surgical team specializes in tumor removal with minimally invasive techniques when possible. We offer both curative and palliative procedures, along with reconstructive options. Our focus is on precision surgery with optimal outcomes and faster recovery.

Immunotherapy & Biologic Treatments

We provide cutting-edge immune-based therapies that harness your body's natural defenses to fight cancer. These innovative treatments include checkpoint inhibitors, CAR T-cell therapy, and cancer vaccines, offering new hope especially for challenging cases.

Clinical Trials & Research Access

As active researchers, we offer eligible patients access to promising new treatments through clinical trials. Our team stays at the forefront of oncology advancements to bring innovative options to our community while contributing to cancer science.

Palliative & Supportive Care

Integrated throughout your treatment journey, our supportive care services address pain management, nutrition, emotional health, and symptom control. Our team works alongside your oncology providers to enhance quality of life during treatment.

Survivorship Care & Long-Term Monitoring

After active treatment, our survivorship program provides comprehensive follow-up care, wellness planning, and monitoring for recurrence. We focus on restoring health, managing long-term effects, and supporting your transition to life beyond cancer.
