Directions & Parking

Our office is conveniently located in the Hope Medical Center building in downtown Portland.

From I-5 North/South: Take exit 300B for City Center. Turn right onto SW 6th Ave. Continue for 0.5 miles, then turn left onto Healing Way.

Public Transportation: We're accessible via MAX Light Rail (Green and Yellow lines) and multiple bus routes. The Healing Way Transit Center is directly across from our building.

Parking Information

Patient parking is available in the covered garage attached to our building. Bring your parking ticket for validation at our front desk. The first 90 minutes are free for patients.

Valet parking is available at the main entrance from 7:30 AM - 4:30 PM Monday-Friday.
