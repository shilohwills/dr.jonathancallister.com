About Dr. Callister

Dedicated to providing exceptional cancer care with compassion and expertise

Professional Background

With over 15 years of specialized experience in oncology, Dr. Jonathan Callister has dedicated his career to advancing cancer treatment and providing exceptional care to patients facing complex diagnoses. He specializes in breast, lung, and colorectal cancers, utilizing the latest evidence-based approaches to develop personalized treatment plans.

Dr. Callister earned his medical degree from Harvard Medical School with honors, followed by residency at Johns Hopkins Hospital. He completed his fellowship in Medical Oncology at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, where he developed expertise in targeted therapies and immunotherapy approaches.

Treatment Philosophy

Dr. Callister believes in treating the whole person, not just the disease. His approach combines cutting-edge medical science with genuine compassion, ensuring each patient receives care tailored to their unique needs. He emphasizes clear communication, empowering patients with knowledge about their condition and involving them in every decision.
